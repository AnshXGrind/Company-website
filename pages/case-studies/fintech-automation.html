Financial Services Automation

Reducing Client Onboarding Time by 70%

How we saved a mid-sized wealth management firm 25+ hours per week using n8n and OpenAI.

At a Glance

  • Client: WealthFlow Partners
  • Industry: FinTech
  • Timeline: 4 Weeks
  • Tech Stack: n8n, OpenAI API, HubSpot, Postgres

The Problem

WealthFlow was growing fast but drowning in paperwork. Every new client required:

  • Manual data entry from PDFs into HubSpot.
  • 4 separate email chains to collect documents.
  • Manual risk assessment scoring by an analyst.

This bottleneck capped their growth at 5 new clients per month.

The Solution

We engineered a fully automated onboarding pipeline:

1. Intelligent Doc Processing

We built a secure portal where clients upload PDFs. An AI agent (GPT-4o) extracts key financial data and validates it instantly against compliance rules.

2. Auto-CRM Sync

Validated data is pushed directly into HubSpot custom fields via n8n webhooks, creating the client profile without human input.

The Outcome

  • ✅ 70% Reduction in onboarding time (from 5 days to 36 hours).
  • ✅ Zero Manual Entry for analysts, freeing up 25 hours/week.
  • ✅ Scalability Unlocked: They now handle 20+ clients/month.
