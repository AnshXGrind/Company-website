E-commerce Support AI

Automating 85% of Customer Support

How an AI Agent helped a DTC brand scale from $1M to $5M without hiring more support staff.

At a Glance

  • Client: Nord Living
  • Industry: Home Goods (DTC)
  • Timeline: 3 Weeks
  • Tech Stack: LangChain, Shopify API, Zendesk

The Problem

Nord Living was experiencing a CX crisis. 40% of orders resulted in "Where is my order?" (WISMO) tickets.

  • Support response time was > 24 hours.
  • Refund processing was manual and error-prone.
  • Support team burnout was high.

The Solution

We deployed a "Tier 1" AI Support Agent that lives inside Zendesk:

1. Instant Order Tracking

The agent connects to Shopify via API. When a user asks "Where is my stuff?", it checks real-time shipping status and replies in natural language instantly.

2. Smart Policy Enforcement

The AI knows the return window. If a customer is eligible, it generates a return label automatically. If not, it politely explains the policy.

The Outcome

  • ✅ 85% Ticket Deflection: Only complex issues reach humans.
  • ✅ Instant Resolution: WISMO tickets solved in < 5 seconds.
  • ✅ $120k/year Saved in projected hiring costs.
